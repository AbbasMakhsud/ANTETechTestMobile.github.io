Best Daily Fantasy Web Sites

When you lose a season-long fantasy league, you basically have to wait until the following season to make up your losses. The experience is completely different when you play at a daily fantasy site. If you lose one contest, you can enter another one the following day. Technically, you can play in several contests each night. Some you’ll win; some you’ll lose.
